Partner Benefits

Why Partner With Britannia?

We offer a transparent, rewarding and supportive partnership programme built on trust and mutual success.

Competitive Commissions

Earn attractive commissions for every student you successfully enrol. Our tiered commission structure rewards high-performing agents with increased rates.

Dedicated Support

Each partner gets a dedicated account manager, priority email and phone support, and access to our agent portal with real-time student tracking.

Marketing Resources

Receive ready-to-use marketing materials, brochures, course details, and co-branded content in your language to help you attract more students.

Visa Support

Our experienced visa team assists your students through the entire student pass application process for Malaysia, ensuring high success rates.

Agent Training

We provide regular online and in-person training sessions to keep agents informed on our programmes, policies, and the latest student visa regulations.

Student Care

From airport pickup to accommodation guidance, we ensure your referred students are fully supported throughout their stay in Kuala Lumpur.
