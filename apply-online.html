Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

Typically 5–7 business days. We review your information and contact you within 24–48 hours of submitting your application.

What is the minimum age requirement?

For Student Visa application, a minimum age of 18 years is mandatory. Our English for Kids program accepts students aged 7–12 years (local students only, no visa required).

Do you provide preparatory or orientation courses?

Yes! We offer pre-course orientation and a placement test on arrival to ensure you are placed in the correct level for your ability.

When does the next course start?

We have new course intakes every month. Download our Course Start Dates PDF or contact admissions for the current schedule.
