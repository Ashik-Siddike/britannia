Simple Process

6-Step Application Process

We've made it easy for you to join Britannia Language Centre. Follow these simple steps to begin your journey.

1

Submit Enquiry

Fill out our online enquiry form or contact us directly via email at leads@britannia.edu.my or WhatsApp.

2

Receive Offer Letter

Our admissions team will review your enquiry and send you an official Offer Letter along with your fee schedule within 2–3 business days.

3

Pay Fees

Pay your tuition fees via Telegraphic Transfer (TT), GIRO, or Flywire (credit/debit card or PayPal) in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR).

4

Submit Documents

Send us all required documents (passport copy, photos, payment proof, etc.) via email. Our team will process your visa application promptly.

5

Visa Approval & Travel

Once your Student Pass is approved, we'll send you the eVAL (electronic Visa Approval Letter). Apply for your single-entry visa at the nearest Malaysian Embassy.

6

Arrive & Start

Arrive in Malaysia, complete your Visa sticker transformation at the Immigration office, and begin your English studies at Britannia! Welcome!

Checklist

Required Documents

Please prepare the following documents before submitting your application.

Student Documents

  • Passport (minimum 18 months validity) — clear copy of all pages including the bio-data page
  • 4 recent passport-sized photos (white background, 3.5cm x 5cm)
  • Highest educational qualification certificate (e.g. IGCSE, O-Level, Diploma, Degree)
  • Bank statement of the past 6 months (minimum RM 10,000 balance)
  • Proof of tuition fee payment (bank transfer receipt or Flywire confirmation)
  • Health declaration — must be in good health (no TB or other communicable diseases)

Sponsor / Guardian Documents

  • Sponsor's passport / national ID copy
  • Sponsor's bank statement (past 6 months, minimum RM 10,000)
  • Sponsor's employment letter or business registration documents
  • Proof of relationship between student and sponsor (e.g. birth certificate)

All documents must be in English. Non-English documents require certified translation.

Payment

Payment Methods

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience. All payments are in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR).

Bank Transfer (TT)

GIRO / Interbank

Interbank GIRO transfer available for local Malaysian bank accounts.

Flywire Online

Pay securely online using Debit/Credit card or PayPal via our Flywire payment portal.
